My Background

I began college years ago and my original plan was to become a dentist. I soon realized that I did not want to pursue this career path any longer, and so I began to transition into the business environment. In doing so, I realized that what I really wanted to do was forge a career in programming in computer science.

Why I Decided to Learn Progamming?

When I realized that a career in the healthcare industry was not for me, I felt lost for a while, I had counted on this one plan for so long, and I did not know what was next. I had completed some business courses during my undergraduate education, and so I decided to change my major to business. In obtaining a part time job as an administrative office assistant, as much as I enjoyed the job and the environment, I still was unsatisfied. I started to learn some programming through free online resources, and upon doing, I immediately knew that this was the career for me. I thoroughly enjoyed what I was doing, and I constantly wanted to know more. I spent a few weeks researching different programming courses, and I am now excited to further my education through Coder Camps.
